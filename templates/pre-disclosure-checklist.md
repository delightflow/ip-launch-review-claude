# Pre-Disclosure Checklist

- Is the current service name distinctive enough for trademark review?
- Have the domain and social handles been checked?
- Which parts are easy to clone from a demo or landing page?
- Which internal steps are technically indispensable?
- What should be shown publicly and what should stay abstracted?
- Is there a founder-ready invention disclosure draft yet?
- Should this move to pre-filing now?
- What needs a patent attorney review before launch?
